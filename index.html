Adopt an Animal

For individuals looking to adopt, motivated by the desire to give a pet a second chance: Be the change an animal needs in their life—and make a difference in your community today. Available animals for adoption include dogs, cats, and bunnies.

Join the Crew

Looking for employment opportunities at Pawsitive Futures? Join our team and work alongside animals every day! We also warmly welcome volunteers to help us foster and find loving homes for our wonderful animal companions.

Get in Touch

Looking to get in touch or ask us a question? No worries! Feel free to reach out by sending us an email, giving us a call, or simply filling out the contact form on our website. Whatever your preference, we've got you covered!

Our Mission

Pawsitive Futures is dedicated to helping pets find loving homes through adoption, while promoting volunteer and fostering opportunities. We also provide essential resources for pet owners. We're here to be the change—and bring joy to some furry faces!

Call to action

Your donations directly benefit both the animals and our organization. One-time or monthly contributions are deeply appreciated—and vital to helping Pawsitive Futures continue making a lasting impact in the community.
